HTML and CSS

This job board is an integral part of a group project I completed during my Front-End development program. Our objective was to create a dynamic tech networking/community website. To explore the full functionality of the website, you can visit it here.

As a team, we initiated the project by designing a wireframe for each section of the website. Here is the wireframe specifically created for the job board.

The focus of this app is to provide a comprehensive job board experience for VIP users of the website. As a VIP user, you gain access to exclusive features, such as saving jobs, submitting applications, researching companies, evaluating your resume against desired job descriptions, managing scheduled interviews, and more.

Developing the job board posed unique challenges, particularly in terms of visual appeal. While most job boards tend to be conventional in design, I aimed to strike a balance between aesthetics and functionality. I am proud of the outcome, as the job board is visually appealing while preserving ample space for practical use.

This project also offered me an opportunity to delve into grid layout, which presented its own set of difficulties. However, the experience was highly educational, and I gained valuable insights from overcoming these challenges.

Be sure to check out the site and the GitHub repository to see it in action.
